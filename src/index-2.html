What is the processing time for funds to appear in my wallet?

Our dedicated managers personally verify each transaction to ensure security. Following your cash-out request, funds are typically deposited into your wallet within one hour.

Is it possible to transfer my voucher to another person?

Absolutely, your voucher is transferable. The recipient can redeem it to transfer the equivalent funds to their wallet, subject to the same terms.

Our vouchers do not come with a fixed expiration date, yet for optimal service, we advise redeeming them within 12 months of purchase.
